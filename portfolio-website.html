The Deep Problem Method™

1

External Problems

The visible challenges your audience articulates: "I can't get enough clients." "My course isn't selling."

"What they say they need help with"

2

Internal Problems

The emotional impact: "I feel like an impostor." "I'm afraid I've wasted years developing expertise no one values."

"What keeps them up at night"

3

Philosophical Problems

The deeper conflict: "Why should someone less qualified succeed while I struggle? Why isn't authentic expertise enough?"

"What violates their sense of how the world should work"

Why This Method Works:

The Repetition & Recognition Effect

When your copy accurately describes problems your audience experiences but couldn't articulate, they'll notice these problems more frequently in their daily lives, creating growing urgency to solve them.

The Chain of Beliefs

People make purchase decisions based on a sequence of beliefs. My copy methodically guides them through this transformation, letting them reach each conclusion themselves.
